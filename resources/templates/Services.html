Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our services

How do I book a delivery?

You can book a delivery by calling our office at +916232690694 or by visiting our office in person.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept cash payments on pickup or delivery. We do not offer online payment options at this time.

How can I track my package?

You can track your order by entering your generated tracking number on our website.

What is your delivery area?

We currently serve all areas within Indore city limits. For deliveries to surrounding areas, please contact us for availability and pricing.

What happens if my package is damaged or lost?

We offer package protection with different coverage levels based on your selected service.

Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?

Standard and Express deliveries are available Monday through Saturday. Overnight delivery is available 7 days a week, including holidays (may incur additional charges).
